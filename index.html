Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DocConnect

How do I book an appointment on DocConnect?

Simply browse our doctor listings, select your preferred doctor, choose an available time slot, and confirm your booking. You'll receive instant confirmation via email and SMS.

Is DocConnect free to use for patients?

Yes, DocConnect is completely free for patients. There are no booking fees or hidden charges. You only pay the doctor's consultation fee as usual.

How do doctors join DocConnect?

Doctors can sign up through our registration process. After verifying their medical credentials, they can create their profile, set their availability, and start accepting appointments.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment through your patient dashboard. Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations to avoid any inconvenience to the doctor.

Is my medical information secure on DocConnect?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and medical information. Your data is never shared with third parties without your consent.
